flickr-machinetag-geo by mncaudill

A bookmarklet to read the machine tags on Flickr photo pages in order to get the associated geo information

Install

To use, drag the following link to your browser bar: flickr-machinetag-geo

A good page to try this out is one Flickr's own Chris Martin's photo pages, such as this one.

  • Foursquare: http://www.flickr.com/photos/hartsell/5196897807/
  • Dopplr: http://www.flickr.com/photos/dx_25/5230609143/
  • last.fm events: http://www.flickr.com/photos/87357254@N00/3841298061/
